## What I'm building

Most of my time right now goes into two platforms I believe will change how software gets built and run:

**[MUXI](https://muxi.org?ref=aroussi&utm_source=aroussi.com&utm_medium=website)** — open-source infrastructure for AI agents. Think Docker, but for agents. The missing layer between raw LLMs and production systems.

**[Cloop](https://cloop.dev?ref=aroussi&utm_source=aroussi.com&utm_medium=website)** — an autonomous engineering control plane. It turns approved issues into pull requests, auto-detects and fixes bugs, and guards against supply-chain attacks. Your engineering team, minus the toil.

Several other ventures are being built on top of these — quietly, for now.
